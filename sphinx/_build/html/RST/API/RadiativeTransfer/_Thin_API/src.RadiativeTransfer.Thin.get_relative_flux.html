calculate optically thin relative flux (some constants are removed)

Parameters

  • _AJI (np.double, np.array, (nLine,) ; scalar) – Einstein A coefficient, [\(s^{-1}\)]

  • _f0 (np.double, np.array, (nLine,); scalar) – Transition line frequency, [\(hz\)]

  • _nj (np.double, np.array, (nLine); scalar) – Population of the upper level of line transition, [\(cm^{-3}\)]

Returns

_rel_flux – relative flux under the assumption of optically thin. [\(erg \; cm^{-3} \; s^{-1}\)]

Return type

np.double, np.array, (_atom.nLine,); scalar

Notes

The absolute flux under the assumption of optically thin 1.

\[F_{ji} = \frac{1}{4 \pi R^{2}} \int_{\Delta V} \epsilon_{ji} dV \quad [erg \; cm^{-2} \; s^{-1}]\]

where the emissivity \(\epsilon_{ji}\) is

\[\epsilon_{ji} = h \nu n_{j} A_{ji} \quad [erg \; cm^{-3} \; s^{-1}]\]

and

\[\epsilon_{ji} = \int_{\nu} \epsilon_{\nu} d \nu = \int_{\nu} h \nu n_{j} A_{ji} \psi d \nu\]
